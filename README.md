# THE PHONEBOOK
The phonebook is a platform which links services with those whom are seeking them.

It is location based giving it a *closer to home* experience which in turn positively affects ones relationship with their community.


## Problem statement.
Time and again we find ourselves needing one service or another. Locating service providers however, especially in less developed areas may prove to be a pain to the public. It is time consuming and hectic, reason being, you may not know directly where to look or probably the ones you know are far or wont be available till next week.

Solution....*the phonebook*

## Solution
The phonebook is a network where service providers are able to connect with those whom seek their services.

*How it works?*
Businesses register and list themselves. This makes their contacts and address available to the public. Users do not have to make accounts in order to use for their personal business.

### Components
#### Services section
This is where service providers will appear in order for users to access

#### Job listing
This is were businesses list job openings to make life easier for those who are job hunting

#### Emergency contacts
This section provides emergency contacts relevant to the location of the user.

#### Store listing
This section lists stores which saves time when people are looking for certain items or want to go shopping.
